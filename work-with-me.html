The Modern Operations Path™

Modern operations means the way your business runs lives in a system, not in a person. The judgment, the language, the offers, the process — written down, structured, and machine-readable — so your team can run it, your buyers can trust it, and AI engines quote it instead of guessing. The Path gets a business there one step at a time. Each step credits into the next.

Where do you start? Answer one question.

  • "I'm not sure my website is actually a problem." The Scan. It's free and it'll tell you in two minutes.
  • "I know it's a problem. I want to understand how bad, from a person, not a tool." The Read.
  • "The website is wrong because the business outgrew it, and I want the real story extracted and on paper." The Map. Skip the Read; the Map includes everything in it and goes deeper.
  • "I want the rebuild." Also the Map. Every build starts there, no exceptions, because that's where honest scope and price come from.
  • "We already work together and I want you in it every month." The Partner.
  • "I've got one operational thing to figure out — an entity, analytics, the tools." A Working Session. One call, no program.
  • "I want you running operations directly, not a productized ladder." The Fractional COO engagement.

How the money works

Each step credits into the next. The Read credits fully toward the Map within 30 days. The Map credits fully toward a build within 60 days. Which means if you end up building, the diagnostics were free, and if you stop anywhere along the way, you own everything produced up to that point and it was built to stand alone.

Nothing here requires a discovery call to learn a price. The diagnostics are priced on their pages; the build's fixed price is the last page of your Map.
