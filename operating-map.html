Two things to know before booking

The credit

100% of the Map fee applies to a build started within 60 days of your memo. If we build, the Map was free. If we don't, you own the memo and it was worth it on its own. The deal is structured so I have no incentive to write a memo that manufactures a build.

The cap

I run at most four Maps a month, because each one gets my actual attention, not a junior's or a template's. If the month is full, you book the next one. The calendar shows real availability.

Questions

  • Is this a website project? No. It's an operating clarity project that usually reveals the website carries a fraction of the business. Some clients proceed to a build; others fix what they can with their existing setup, using the memo as the spec. Both are fine outcomes.
  • We already have brand strategy docs. How is this different? Brand strategy describes how you want to be seen. The Operating Map documents how you actually run, then derives the story from that. It's the difference between aspiration and extraction.
  • Can my partner / CMO / ops lead join the sessions? Yes, and usually should. The founder is required; up to two others are welcome. More than that and the sessions turn into meetings, and meetings don't extract anything.
  • What happens to the AI and automation findings? Session two surfaces where tooling and automation would pay off, and the memo includes a ranked list with rough costs. If a build happens, that work gets scoped in. If not, the list stands on its own.
  • What if I just want the build? Every build starts with a Map. It's how I scope honestly and how you avoid rebuilding the wrong thing. No exceptions, including for people who offer to pay more to skip it.
