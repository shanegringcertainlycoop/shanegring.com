What I'm looking for

Most site audits check conversion mechanics. Button placement, headline formulas, page speed. Useful, narrow, and not what this is.

The Read looks at three things:

  • Does the site carry your operating logic? How you actually work, who you actually serve, what actually makes you different. Most sites carry a summary written years ago by someone who no longer works there.
  • Can machines read it? Structure, not just content. Whether your pages are built in a way that lets search and AI engines extract, understand, and quote the real story. Most aren't, and it's invisible until you look.
  • Does the story hold together? One business, one message, across every page. Or five eras of the company layered on top of each other.
